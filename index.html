Best Places for San Diego Sunsets

San Diego is known as America's Finest City! As should be expected, it is also known of some of the most beautiful sunsets in the world! Here are a few we think you'll like!




Sunset Cliffs

Sunset cliffs is located in the heart of Ocean Beach. Hippies welcome!

Coronado Island

Coronado Island is located bit south of downtown and over a bridge with a great veiw. Prepare to feel fancy!

Mt Soledad

Mt Soledad is located in the southern section of La Jolla. A fantastic monument to fallen soldiers!
